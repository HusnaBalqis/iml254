College Buddies

Always keep your circle small. Be independent, and don't be afraid if you are alone. Don't put your trust in people too much. Be with someone willing to accept who you are. Enjoy your college moments and make plans with your classmates. Keep in mind that you will find a lot of different attitudes once you are in your college years. Enjoy your college era because you will be missing it once you start your working life.

Secondary Buddies

Secondary friends are known to be someone who understands you better. Memories made in school are the best feelings ever. You get to experience feelings such as skipping class, sports day, entrepreneurship day, teacher's day, and more. We can't deny that secondary is the best we had been experiencing.
